About Us

Crafting Moments, One Cup at a Time

Founded on a love for authentic flavors, our shop is a sanctuary for coffee enthusiasts and dessert lovers alike. We specialize in unique blends like our signature Dalgona and traditional Lungo, paired perfectly with artisanal treats like our homemade Tiramisu.

Our mission is simple: to create a space where community and quality meet. We invite you to sit back, take a sip, and enjoy the rich aromas of our specially roasted beans.
